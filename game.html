Roll It and Defeat !!!

Rules:

  • Player1(Red) starts the game and rolls the dice.
  • With each roll the number on the dice face gets added to the total score of current player.
  • This continues until he gets 1 on the dice.
  • After this its the Player2's(Blue) turn.
  • This continues untill any player reaches (25 in easy mode/50 in medium mode/100 in hard mode) points.
  • But if a player gets the unlucky three his score if reduced to half.
  • The first player to reach maximum score is the winner.
